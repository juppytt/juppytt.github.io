    Writings on Security Research and Graduate Life

    Hello, my name is Juhee Kim, and I am a PhD student at CompSec Lab, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Seoul National University. I am advised by Byoungyoung Lee.

    My research interests span a broad spectrum of software security issues and finding practical solutions. I’m interested in Linux kernel, Web browsers, and ML systems.
